Chameleon

Interesting Facts about Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree brances

Camel

Interesting Facts about Camels

  • Camels can either have one hump or two humps depending on what type they are
  • Camels are used in deserts for long periods of time because they don't need much water
  • Camels can weigh up to 1500 pounds and be up to 7 feet in height

Cicada

Interesting Facts about Cicadas

  • Cicadas come out once every 17 years from underground after breeding
  • They stay for a couple of months but then breed before they die when they go underground
  • Cicadas are extremely loud and can be very annoying

Panda

Interesting Facts about Pandas

  • Pandas eat bamboo
  • Pandas usually have black and white fur
  • Pandas are usually very big
